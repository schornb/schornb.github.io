Hi 👋! I broadly work on problems at the intersection of neuroengineering, brain–computer interfaces (BCIs), and translational neuroscience, with a focus on restoring motor function and autonomy in individuals with neurological injury. My work spans intracortical and ECoG-based decoding, real-time neural signal processing, neurofeedback, and closed-loop rehabilitation systems, including wearable soft robotics and spinal cord stimulation–based BCIs.

I am a research engineer at .NeuroRestore advised by Dr. Henri Lorach. Before this, I graduated as the top student of the Brown CE department, where I was blessed with amazing mentors: Drs. Leigh Hochberg, Arto Nurrikko, George Kondiaris, .
